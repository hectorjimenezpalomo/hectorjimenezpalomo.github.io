Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Delete Account inside the App. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings and cancel LibrosApp Pro from there. We cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf.

I was charged but didn't receive my coins.
Coins are credited automatically after a successful purchase. If you didn't receive them, try restarting the App. If the issue persists, email us with your purchase receipt and we will resolve it promptly.

How do I request a refund?
All payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. To request a refund, please contact Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com or Google Play support directly. For purchases older than the platform's refund window, contact us at privacidad@librosapp.es and we will review your case.

How do I export or download my data?
Go to Settings → Export Data inside the App. You can also submit a data portability request by emailing us.

I forgot my password.
On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes.

The App is crashing or not loading.
Try force-closing the App and reopening it. If the problem persists, try deleting and reinstalling the App — your data is stored in the cloud and will be restored when you log back in.

How do I report a user or inappropriate content?
Tap the three-dot menu on any review or user profile and select "Report". You can also email us directly with details.

Purchases and Refunds Policy

All in-app purchases (coin packages and subscriptions) are governed by the platform through which they were made (Apple App Store or Google Play). LibrosApp does not have access to your payment information.

  • Virtual coins purchased are non-refundable except where required by applicable law
  • Subscription fees are non-refundable for the current billing period, except as required by law
  • If you believe you have been charged in error, contact us and we will investigate

Account Deletion and Data Requests

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. You can do this directly in the App (Settings → Delete Account) or by emailing privacidad@librosapp.es. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with GDPR requirements.
