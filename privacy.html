Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026  ·  Effective: March 26, 2026

LibrosApp is operated as an individual developer project under the name LibrosApp, based in Spain. We are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Spain's Organic Law on Data Protection (LOPDGDD).

1. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

  • Name: LibrosApp
  • Country: Spain
  • Contact: privacidad@librosapp.es

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account data: email address, username, full name (optional), profile photo (optional), and biography (optional)
  • Reading data: books added, reading progress, reading sessions, notes, reviews, and yearly reading goals
  • Activity data: reading streaks, achievements, daily challenges, and coin balance
  • Social data: users you follow and who follow you, reactions to reviews, and follow requests
  • Device data: push notification token (only if you enable reading reminders)
  • Purchase data: in-app purchase transactions processed through Apple App Store or Google Play (we do not store payment card details)
  • Technical data: anonymised crash reports and error logs via Sentry

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of a contract: processing necessary to provide the App's core features (account management, library, reading tracking)
  • Legitimate interests: anonymised analytics to improve the App and prevent abuse
  • Consent: push notifications (you may withdraw consent at any time in device Settings)
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable laws and tax regulations related to in-app purchases

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your sessions
  • To display your library, reading progress, statistics, and achievements
  • To send reading reminders (only if enabled in Settings)
  • To display your public profile to other users of the App
  • To process in-app purchases and credit virtual coins to your account
  • To send social notifications (new followers, reactions to your reviews)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • To improve the App through anonymised crash and error reporting

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data — including your profile, library, reading sessions, notes, reviews, and social connections — is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytical purposes.
